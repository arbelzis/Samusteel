  • Wooden Handle

  • Sharpener Included

Samurai Basic knives are great for those who like to cut and slice for their families and want to get into the knives game. The knives are made of strong Tool steel and has wooden handle for stable grip.

Samurai Pro

Samurai Pro knives are great for those who want to take their slicing game to the next level. The knives are made of strong and high density Carbon steel and each knife has a metal handle for more natural grip and feel for the blade.

Samurai Max knives are great for professionals and chefs. The knives are made of Stainless steel which is super strong and sharp and each knife has a premium wooden handle for better grip. The blade can also sharpen itself while using it.
